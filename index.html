5 Accounting Skills Demonstrated

Each skill automates a real accounting workflow end-to-end.

🗃

Receipt Processing

OCR extraction of date, vendor, amounts, and tax from receipt images. Auto-generates categorized expense spreadsheets.

Before: ~2 hours → After: ~10 min
📁

Expense Categorization

Automatically sorts expenses into categories (travel, meals, supplies, software) and flags items over $500 for review.

Before: ~45 min → After: ~3 min
📄

Invoice Management

Extracts vendor names, amounts, due dates, and invoice numbers. Highlights overdue invoices and generates tracking sheets.

Before: ~1 hour → After: ~5 min
💶

Bank Reconciliation

Matches bank statement transactions to GL entries, cleans vendor names, and flags unmatched items for review.

Before: ~3 hours → After: ~15 min
📊

Monthly Reporting

Compiles category breakdowns, month-over-month comparisons, unpaid invoice lists, and flagged items into a summary report.

Before: ~3 hours → After: ~15 min

How Claude Cowork Skills Work

1

Drop Files

Add receipts, bank statements, invoices, or GL exports to your Cowork project folder.

2

Activate Skill

Select a saved skill or type a natural language prompt describing the task.

3

AI Executes

Claude reads files, processes data, categorizes, reconciles, and generates output files automatically.

4

Review & Approve

Review flagged items, approve the output, and export to Excel or your accounting software.
